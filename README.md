## Abstract
This paper introduces Resonance Substrate Theory through the Mathematical Resonance Model of Ether (MRME). Ether is defined as a universal substrate structured by resonance fields, with energy-resonance relations governing the emergence of physical constants, matter, and consciousness. MRME establishes resonance equations, dispersion relations, and predictive mass ratios, positioning consciousness at the resonance peak of the cosmos. Worked examples demonstrate predictive precision, including electron/muon mass ratios and resonance scaling laws. The model integrates philosophical parallels (Plato, Bohm, Jung) while remaining falsifiable through collider searches, EEG coherence studies, and gravitational interferometry.
